There's no dial tone

We need a dial tone in order to establish the phone connection from your computer. Please make sure that these conditions are true:
  • The phone line is available for use.
  • The phone cord between your computer and the phone outlet is connected correctly, as outlined in your computer's documentation.




    • If you need to move your computer closer to a phone outlet, turn off the power first. When you restart your computer we'll pick up where we left off.

    If you receive this message a second time, you can skip this step and we'll remind you later.

To try to connect again, click Redial. To skip this step, click Skip.
